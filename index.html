The Ordinal Edition ●
100

Inscribed on Bitcoin.
Redeemable for the hand-bound chapbook.

~$100 USD  ·  100 inscriptions  ·  Never reprinted

Each of the 100 Ordinal inscriptions contains the complete story — the full text, the cover art, and the interactive animation that plays during the dream sequence. Inscribed permanently on BSV Blockchain. Not a file on a server somewhere. The story itself, on-chain, forever.

Hold it as a digital artefact. Or burn it.

Burning your Ordinal is an irreversible act that retires the digital inscription from existence and triggers fulfilment of a hand-bound physical chapbook — numbered to match, shipped to you personally by the author.

How many chapbooks will exist? That depends entirely on how many holders choose to burn. It could be one. It could be all one hundred. The uncertainty is the point to this exercise in collectible rarities.

The Burn → Redeem Mechanic

01 Mint your Ordinal inscription on 3Dordi or buy one through chefmyklove.com/we're_naked/ordinals
02 When ready to redeem, sign in with your wallet at chefmyklove.com/redeem to begin the process. The burn is permanent and public.
03 Ensure your burn transaction hash and shipping address are correct at chefmyklove.com/redeem
04 The author verifies on-chain, hand-binds your copy and ships the physical chapbook to your provided address.
05 Your chapbook arrives numbered to match the Ordinal it replaced — with a QR code linking to its on-chain provenance forever

Once burned, that numbered digital inscription is gone. The chapbook and another unique collectible (think bonus stuff - like a copy of the OG draft of the story) are what remains.
